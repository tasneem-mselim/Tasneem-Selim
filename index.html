About Me

Tasneem Selim

I am a researcher and Assistant Lecturer in Computer Science at Alexandria University, Egypt. My MSc focused on deep learning for student engagement recognition — the resulting publication in IEEE Access has accumulated 95+ citations.

My research spans Computer Vision, Graph Neural Networks, Generative AI, Multimodal Learning, Multi-agent AI, ASR, LLMs, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and AI for Medicine, with a consistent emphasis on lightweight deployable models and robust evaluation frameworks.

Actively seeking PhD opportunities in CV, GNN, multimodal learning, multi-agent, LLMs, and medical AI.
